About Us

TinyTables is a cozy culinary haven where the timeless flavors of Greece meet modern sophistication. Our passion is to bring the warmth and joy of Greek dining to your table in a relaxed yet refined atmosphere.

Appetizers

Tzatziki with Olive Oil

Creamy Greek yogurt blended with cucumbers, garlic, fresh dill, and extra virgin olive oil. A refreshing start inspired by traditional Greek kitchens.

Dolmades

Tender grape leaves stuffed with aromatic rice, pine nuts, and herbs, served with lemon wedges to awaken your palate.

Spanakopita

Flaky filo pastry filled with a savory mixture of spinach, feta cheese, and fresh herbs, baked to golden perfection.

Main Course

Moussaka

Layers of eggplant, ground lamb, potatoes, and béchamel sauce creating the iconic, hearty dish of Greek origin.

Souvlaki

Skewers of marinated pork or chicken grilled over open flame and served with fresh pita bread and tzatziki dip.

Grilled Sea Bass

Fresh whole sea bass marinated in lemon, olive oil, and oregano, grilled to tender perfection — a taste of the Greek islands.

Gigantes Plaki

Giant butter beans baked in rich tomato sauce with fresh herbs and olive oil — a vegetarian favorite steeped in Greek tradition.

Desserts

Baklava

Layers of crisp filo dough filled with chopped nuts and sweetened with honey syrup — a classic Greek indulgence.

Loukoumades

Golden honey-soaked doughnuts sprinkled with cinnamon and crushed walnuts — a delightful sweet bite.

Greek Yogurt with Honey & Walnuts

Creamy and thick Greek yogurt topped with wildflower honey and a sprinkle of toasted walnuts for a light and wholesome finish.

Beverages

Greek Coffee

Strong and aromatic coffee served in small cups, with a thick foam layer, brewed the traditional Greek way.

Ouzo

Anise-flavored aperitif essential to Greek culture, served chilled to complement your dining experience.

Santorini Assyrtiko

A crisp white wine from the volcanic island of Santorini, bursting with citrus and mineral notes.

Mountain Tea

A calming herbal infusion made from wild herbs gathered from the mountains of Greece, naturally caffeine-free.
